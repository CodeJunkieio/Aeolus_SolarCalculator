How to use the Solar calculator


Here is a step-by-step guide on how to calculate the required amount for power for all your appliances using the solar calculator!


Requirements for Calculation


All you need to get started making your calculations are:


  • Appliance E.g TV
  • Number of appliance - How many TVs do you intend to use
  • Wattage - How many Watts of power does the TV require (This can usually be found on the back/rear of the appliance)
  • Duration of use - How long do you intend to use the TV for?

That's All!!!


How It Works


Step 1: Click on the "Solar Calculator" button on the Homepage

Step 2: Fill in the form as appropriate, inputting the "Appliance Name", "Quantity", "Wattage" and "Duration of Use(hours)" and then click on "Add Appliance". As many appliances as needed can be added.

Step 3: The total consumption is displayed at the top of the table with the individual items below it. You can remove appliances by using the "Del" button. The app allows you to expport your calculations to your email address also! Just click the "export calculations to main" button

Step 4: Fill in the pop-up form, inputting your Full name and Email address and click "Send Message"

And that's all!! Your power requirements will be calculated instantly! And sent to your mailbox if you wish!!



Solar Calculator also gives you the additional ability to sign in so you can access your calculations whenever you log in.
